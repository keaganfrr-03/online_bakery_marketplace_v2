About Us

Connecting Bakeries and Customers Through Technology

Empowering Bakeries with Technology

At Crumb & Co., we empower local bakeries to thrive in the digital age. Traditional bakeries often face challenges like limited customer access, sold-out favorites, reliance on cash payments, and lack of online presence. Our platform bridges this gap by providing an easy-to-use online marketplace where customers can discover, order, and enjoy fresh bakery products with convenience. Founded by a passionate team of six students with expertise in business, software, AI, IT, and supply chain, Crumb & Co. unites artisanal skill with modern technology to strengthen the connection between bakeries and their communities.
